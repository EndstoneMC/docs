### BossBar [#endstone.boss.BossBar]

Represents a boss bar that is displayed to players.

**Methods:**

- [**add_flag**](#endstone.boss.BossBar.add_flag) – Adds an optional flag to this boss bar.
- [**add_player**](#endstone.boss.BossBar.add_player) – Adds the player to this boss bar causing it to display on their screen.
- [**has_flag**](#endstone.boss.BossBar.has_flag) – Checks whether this boss bar has the passed flag set.
- [**remove_all**](#endstone.boss.BossBar.remove_all) – Removes all players from this boss bar.
- [**remove_flag**](#endstone.boss.BossBar.remove_flag) – Removes an existing flag on this boss bar.
- [**remove_player**](#endstone.boss.BossBar.remove_player) – Removes the player from this boss bar causing it to be removed from their screen.
